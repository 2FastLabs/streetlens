StreetLens Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 19, 2025
Last Updated: December 19, 2025

Introduction

StreetLens ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the StreetLens mobile application (the "App").

Information We Access

Location Information

  • On-Device Only: Your location is used solely on your device to provide turn-by-turn navigation and detect when you arrive at points of interest.
  • Never Transmitted: Your location data is never sent to our servers or any third parties.
  • No Storage: We do not store your location history.

Audio Data

  • Audio Playback: We track audio playback progress locally on your device to resume tours where you left off
  • No Recording: We do not record or access any audio from your device's microphone

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your location data
  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers

Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Tour bookmarks and preferences
  • Audio playback progress
  • Downloaded tour content for offline use

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by deleting the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple according to their privacy policy.

Your Rights

  • You can control location access through your device's Settings app
  • You can delete all local data by removing the App

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
